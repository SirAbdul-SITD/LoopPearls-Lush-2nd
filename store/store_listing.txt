Huecraft: Color Flood Puzzle

SHORT DESCRIPTION (max 80 chars):
Flood the whole board into one color before you run out of moves! 150 levels.

FULL DESCRIPTION:
🌊 FLOOD. FILL. CONQUER. 🌊

Starting from the top-left corner, pick a color and your region spreads to swallow every touching cell of that color. Chain your choices to flood the entire board into a single color — before your moves run out.

🎨 HOW TO PLAY
• Tap a color from the palette
• Your corner region floods to that color, absorbing neighbors
• Plan colors that capture the most cells each turn
• Flood the whole board within the move limit to win

✨ FEATURES
• 150 levels: 8×8, 11×11 and 14×14 boards with 4–6 colors
• Every level is winnable within its limit — budget set from a real solve
• Move counter and live region highlight
• Bright, satisfying color visuals and a chill ambient soundtrack
• Haptic feedback
• 100% offline, no ads, no purchases, zero data collection
