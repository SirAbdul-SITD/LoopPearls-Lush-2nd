APP TITLE (max 50 chars):
Loop Pearls: Masyu Logic

SHORT DESCRIPTION (max 80 chars):
Draw one loop: straight through white pearls, turning on black. 150 puzzles!

FULL DESCRIPTION:
⚪ WHITE STRAIGHT. BLACK TURNS. ⚫

Thread a single closed loop through the grid that obeys the pearls. At a white pearl the loop runs straight through but must bend just before or after. At a black pearl the loop must turn — and run straight on both sides. Read the pearls and find the one loop that satisfies them all.

🔄 HOW TO PLAY
• Tap between two cells to draw a loop segment
• Tap again to cross it out, once more to clear
• White pearl: go straight through, turn in a neighbor cell
• Black pearl: turn here, go straight on both sides
• Form one single closed loop to win

✨ FEATURES
• 150 levels: 6×6, 8×8 and 10×10 grids
• Every puzzle is built from a real loop — always solvable
• Elegant white and black pearl tokens
• Glowing jade loop on an ink-wash board
• Calming soundtrack and haptic feedback
• 100% offline, no ads, no purchases, zero data collection

Also known as Masyu or "Pearl Necklace" — one of the most elegant loop puzzles ever designed. Two pearl rules, one loop, endless satisfying deduction.
